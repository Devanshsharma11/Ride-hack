Drone Racing

In drone racing, pilots compete against each other to complete a set course in the fastest time possible. Courses are often set up with obstacles, such as hoops, gates, and flags, that pilots must navigate through while piloting their drones at high speeds.

Drone Combat

Drone combat games involve drones equipped with non-lethal weapons, such as nets, lasers, or foam projectiles, engaging in aerial combat with each other. Pilots aim to disable or "shoot down" their opponents' drones while avoiding damage to their own. These games require strategic thinking, quick reflexes, and precise control to outmaneuver opponents and emerge victorious.

Drone Freestyle

In drone freestyle games, pilots showcase their creativity and skill by performing intricate aerial maneuvers and tricks with their drones. Pilots choreograph routines that include flips, rolls, spins, and other acrobatic maneuvers, often set to music. The goal is to impress judges and spectators with the complexity, smoothness, and style of their maneuvers..
